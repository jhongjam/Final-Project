About the author

Hello, welcome to my site. I, the author, am named Jimmy Jhong. For my entire life, I have had an intense passion for animals. However, in all my studies and observations I have grown a particular affinity towards animals of the Canis lupus familiaris variety, or more commonly known as dogs. I have spent my life growing up around members of the Canis lupus familiaris variety, and I have come to grow even stronger affinities towards certain pedigrees. I would like to elaborate on the varieties I have particular interest in within this page.
